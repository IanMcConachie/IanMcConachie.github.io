About Me

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Ian McConachie and I am a student studying math and computer science at the University of Oregon. My expected graduation date is in June 2023, after which I plan to pursue a career in technical writing. I am passionate about technology and science, but also love communicating with people especially through writing. It is my goal to spread interest and curiosity in technology so that I can share what I find so fascinating with the world.

Outside the realm of technical writing, I also care deeply about volunteering. I have worked with organizations such as the Oregon Justice Resource Center, FOOD for Lane County, and the Oregon Food Bank. I believe it is important for every individual to try to make a positive impact on the world around them and I am always looking for new ways to help out. In addition to volunteering, my hobbies include photography, hiking/backpacking, and table tennis.
